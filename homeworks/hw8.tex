\begin{hwexercise}
Pick your favorite even power series $Q(x)$ with constant term $1$ (you're not allowed to pick $Q(x) = 1 + x^2$ or $Q(x) = \frac{x}{\tanh(x)}$). Your coefficients can be in any $\Q$-algebra. You have now defined a genus $\phi_Q$!
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Write down the first few terms of your multiplicative series $K_1$, $K_2$, $K_3$, etc. Is there a general formula?
    \item Find the logarithm of $Q(x)$ (recall this is the $g(y)$ from the notes).
    \item Write out the values of your genus on even-dimensional projective spaces, at least the first few $\phi_Q([\CP^2])$, $\phi_Q(\CP^4])$, etc.
\end{enumerate}
\end{hwexercise}
